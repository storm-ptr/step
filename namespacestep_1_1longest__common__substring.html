Find the longest string that is a substring of two strings.

The two strings are padded with unique terminators. Time complexity O((N+M)*log(N+M)), space complexity O(N+M), where: N = std::distance(first1, last1) and M = std::distance(first2, last2). A suffix tree with optional parameter is used under the hood:
