Find the longest subsequence present in two sequences.

A subsequence is a sequence that appears in the same relative order, but not necessarily contiguous. Time complexity O(N*M), space complexity O(min(N,M)), where: N = std::distance(first1, last1), M = std::distance(first2, last2).
